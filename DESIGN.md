# DESIGN.md — decade-lens

Brand cheat sheet for the composition. Source of truth: `~/knowledge/context/brand/design-system.md` (extracted from agilelens.com) + the HarvardXR keynote accent rotation (`~/knowledge/projects/harvardxr-keynote/overview.md`).

## Canvas
- 1920 × 1080 @ 30 fps, 50s (1500 frames)
- Dark mode throughout. Never light backgrounds.

## Keynote accent rotation (dots, year chips)
From the HarvardXR keynote map finale. Brightened for small-element legibility on `#101011` (deep purple `#3500A7` is near-invisible at dot scale):

| Accent | Years | Hex used |
|---|---|---|
| teal | 2016, 2019, 2023 | `#00C98D` |
| purple | 2017, 2021, 2024 | `#7C4DFF` |
| amber | 2018, 2022, bonus 2026 | `#FFB84D` |
| rose | 2020, 2025 | `#FF5C8A` |

## Typography
- Manrope (Google Fonts, 200–800). Display text at weight 300 — never heavy weights for large text.
- Category tags: teal border pill, small caps, letterspaced (design-system "category tag" pattern).
- Body/subtitles: `#C5C5C5`, weight 300–400.

## Tone
Premium, sophisticated, immersive. One idea per beat, generous breathing room. Magenta/purple gradient used sparingly, as highlights only. No hype language (brand-identity.md: no "revolutionary / disruptive / game-changing").

## Motifs
- Lens/orbit ring (logo motif) — closing wordmark and the beat-7 constellation.
- Monocle constellation — 10 year-dots on a ring + cord to a 2026 bonus dot, quoting the keynote's map finale.
- Wireframe DK1 headset line-drawing — beat 1 origin story.
